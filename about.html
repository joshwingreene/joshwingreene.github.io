Welcome!

The name's Joshwin and I'm a Product Designer who recently graduated from Flatiron School's UX/UI Design Immersive (Jan 2020). My story as a product and user experience designer began when I started to spearhead the design of ScholarDev App's mobile bus schedule, called Fresno Transit Free That application was launched in 2014, garnered over 15,000 downloads on Google Play  and had a 4/5 star rating as of 2016. While designing and developing Fresno Transit Free, I cultivated a strong passion for the creation of great user experiences and design in general.

Here’s a picture of my team and I when we tied for People’s Choice in a local hackathon called 59DaysofCode:

I originally graduated from University of California, Irvine with a B.S. in Software Engineering in 2016. However, after further exploring user experience design and doing some UI/UX and frontend development work after college, I realized that user experience design was my true calling. I continue to keep up my software engineering skills to an extent in order to be able to communicate with engineers and pursue technological entrepreneurial endeavors on the side.

💻 View my Past Development Work

At the end of 2018, I entered into 59DaysofCode again with a product that would ultimately compete against Pocket  Instapaper  and Feedly after I identified a need in the Save-for-Later space while conducting an independent user experience case study in 2017 to help me get my foot in the door as a UX Designer. I served as the project lead, product designer, and co-developer of the product’s frontend. We ended up coming in second place. You can sign up for the beta by using the following link: Korey.

After completing this competition, I decided to put Korey on the backburner and go all-in with pursuing a job in user experience design by enrolling in Flatiron School's UX/UI Design Immersive program.

As you can see, I'm someone who can bridge the gap between design and development. My goal is to reach the upper echelons of what it means to be a great Product Designer.

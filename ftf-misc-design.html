App Icon

This icon is heavily based on a bus graphic that is a part of the bus stop signs of the Fresno Area Express. This icon was used as the basis for the Android and iOS app icons. I generated the Android app icon using this tool whereas the iOS app icon was outsourced to the graphic artist that we were working with at the time. She also helped me fine-tune this image.
